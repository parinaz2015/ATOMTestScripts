(1 fermi \(= 10^{-15}\) m). Values of the magnetic dipole moment are in units of \(\mu_N\) (nuclear magneton) whereas values of the electric quadrupole moment are in units of b
(barn, 1b\(= 10^{-28}\) m\(^2\))
